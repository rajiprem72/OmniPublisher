# OmniPublisher

**Create Once. Publish Anywhere.**

OmniPublisher is a modern, extensible social media publishing platform designed to help content creators, businesses, educators, and marketers publish content across multiple social media platforms from a single application.

Instead of manually uploading the same content to different platforms, OmniPublisher allows users to create content once and distribute it to multiple destinations through configurable publishing channels.

---

## Vision

Our vision is to build a universal publishing platform where users can:

* Create content once
* Select a publishing channel
* Publish to multiple social media platforms with a single click

OmniPublisher is designed to be modular, scalable, and easy to extend as new social media platforms emerge.

---

## Key Features

### Publishing Channels

Create multiple channels, each with its own branding, platforms, destinations, hashtags, and publishing preferences.

Examples:

* Yennam Pol Vazhvu
* Tarot Shakti by Nisha
* Divine Connectionz
* Devamritham

---

### Multi-Platform Publishing

Publish content to supported platforms, including:

* WhatsApp Groups
* WhatsApp Status
* Telegram
* Facebook
* Instagram
* X (Twitter)
* Threads
* LinkedIn
* Pinterest
* YouTube Community
* Website
* Email

Additional platforms can be added through the modular architecture.

---

### Content Types

OmniPublisher is designed to support:

* Videos
* Images
* Text Posts
* Links
* Documents
* Carousels (Future)

---

### Publish History

Maintain a complete history of all published content, including:

* Date and Time
* Publishing Channel
* Platforms Used
* Status
* Success / Failure Logs

---

### Scheduling (Planned)

Schedule posts to be published automatically at a future date and time.

---

### AI Features (Planned)

Future versions may include:

* AI Caption Generator
* AI Hashtag Generator
* AI Description Generator
* AI Translation
* AI Content Suggestions
